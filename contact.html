What is copy trading?

Copy trading is a trading strategy where one trader’s position or portfolio is copied by another when opened or closed. It is suitable for traders who are new to the stock market and need more expertise, time, and commitment to analyze the market. It can be done either manually or automatically. The traders whose position is copied are typically experienced and backed by years of knowledge. Therefore, a trader can choose the amount they want to invest.
